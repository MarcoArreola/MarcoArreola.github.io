Data Analyst at DENLIN

During my tenure as a data analyst, a significant part of my experience revolved around working on a mobile application project called "Veloz Mandados," which focused on food and errand delivery services. The app was launched in several cities around Mexico. My role involved extracting information from a NoSQL database, specifically MongoDB, which housed data from different regions across Mexico, as well as the sub-divisions within each city.


In order to derive meaningful insights, I performed various tasks such as data cleaning, data aggregation, statistical analysis, and data visualization. By applying these techniques, I was able to identify patterns, trends, and key metrics that provided valuable insights into user behavior, market demand, and operational performance.


Once I had gathered valuable information, I prepared comprehensive presentations for my superiors and colleagues within the company. These presentations highlighted the important findings and included recommendations to support decision-making processes. By effectively communicating the outcomes of my analyses, I ensured that stakeholders were well-informed and equipped with the necessary insights to make data-driven decisions.
